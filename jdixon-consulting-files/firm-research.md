# James Dixon Architect PC - Complete Research

## Firm Overview

James Dixon Architect PC is a full-service architectural practice handling projects from initial concept through construction administration for primarily residential clients. The firm emphasizes bespoke design, careful detailing, and site-specific responses, especially for rural properties in the Hudson Valley/Berkshires and urban apartments in New York and Portland.

Founded in 1999, the practice positions itself around long-term client relationships and repeat work for families as their needs evolve. It operates as a boutique studio with principal-led design and hands-on project involvement.
